Community for Every Stage of Life

We believe people grow best when they are known, supported, and walking alongside others in a similar season of life. Our ministries create space for friendships, honest conversations, and learning how faith connects to everyday life. Wherever you are on your journey, there is a place here for you.

"From him the whole body, joined and held together by every supporting ligament, grows and builds itself up in love, as each part does its work." — Ephesians 4:16

Children’s Ministry

Our Children’s Ministry is a safe and joyful environment where kids learn about God’s love in ways they can understand. Through stories, songs, activities, and caring leaders, children discover that church is a place where they belong. We partner with families to help kids grow in faith from an early age. Open to children from nursery through elementary school. Every child is welcomed and cared for by trained volunteers.

We'd love to see you

  • Kids head to Sunday School after the music & singing
  • Family Sunday every last Sunday of the month
  • VBS: Vacation Bible School every Summer

"Start children off on the way they should go, and even when they are old they will not turn from it." — Proverbs 22:6

Youth Ministry

Youth Ministry is a place for students to ask questions, build strong friendships, and explore faith in a real and supportive setting. We talk about life, purpose, and following Jesus in ways that connect with school, family, and daily challenges. Students are encouraged to grow in confidence, character, and compassion. Open to grade 6, junior high, and high school students.
